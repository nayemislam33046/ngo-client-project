Event

Winter Relief Aid in Gaza

Severe winter has begun in Gaza. Through the initiative of Alhaj Shamsul Hoque Foundation and the donations and support of the people of Bangladesh, thick tarpaulins, warm clothing, and food supplies are being distributed in Gaza. Emergency relief and mosque reconstruction activities are ongoing in Khan Yunis

ASHF Stands by the Family of Martyr Saiful Islam

Alhaj Shamsul Hoque Foundation has provided 100,000 Taka from its own fund to the family of Martyr Advocate Saiful Islam Alif. The bereaved family includes a 2.5-year-old child, a pregnant wife, and an unborn baby. A fundraising campaign is underway to raise 10 million Taka for their future security.
